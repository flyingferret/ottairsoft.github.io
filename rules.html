CALL YOUR HITS

When hit Shout “HIT!” and take out and display your “Death Rag”. Please note ricochet hits (even your own) count and gun hits take the gun out of action. Remember Dead men do not speak.

Eye Protection must always be worn unless within the Safe Zone or directed by a marshal. While in the Safe Zone all weapons to be unloaded or Holstered.

Headshots are discouraged but accepted, only if no other valid target.

SURRENDER Rule If you are 10 feet or closer to an opposing player or players, you can offer them the opportunity to surrender.

Yell "SURRENDER" If they say, "I surrender", they are out. If they make any move as to engage in battle, after your offer, you may shoot them.

Games will start with 1 Whistle Blast, end with 2 Whistle Blasts and Pause with 3 Whistle Blasts. When a pause happens apply your safety and remain where you are, the game will then be restarted with 1 Whistle Blast.

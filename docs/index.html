About Us

Over The Top Airsoft Adventures is a UKARA registered airsoft site in Anglesey, North Wales.

We have a large 300 acre woodland area, split into 3 different areas. As well as a number of differnt buildings with lots of rooms to clear and places to hide.

A number of different teams call OTT home - these include: The Dog Soldiers, The Spartans, The Wolf Legion, The Valley Hawks, The Avengers, The Ardvarks, Alpha Squad, The Gogs and Bangor Airsoft Society.

Wether you like outdoor games or close quarter battle, you'll have a great time at OTT.

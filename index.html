About Us

Over The Top Airsoft Adventures (OTT for short) is a UKARA registered airsoft site in Anglesey, North Wales.

We have a large 300 acre woodland area, split into different areas. As well as a number of buildings - some of which have multiple floors - with lots of rooms to clear and places to hide.

A number of different teams call OTT home - these include: The Dog Soldiers, The Spartans, The Wolf Legion, The Valley Hawks, The Avengers, The Ardvarks, Alpha Squad, The Gogs and Bangor Airsoft Society.

There is free parking available onsite. As well as a shop where you can buy refreshments and army surplus gear.

Whether you like outdoor games or close quarter battle, you'll have a great time at OTT!

Other Activities

Airsoft is not the only thing we do at OTT though...

We also offer: archery classes; bushcraft classes; and paintball games. You can even camp here over night.

Drop us an email (ottairsoft.club@mail.com) or give us a call (07895478634) for more information.

From The A55 North Wales Expressway

If you're coming from the A55:

  1. Cross the Britania Bridge and come off at junction 8 onto the A5052 towards Benllech/Amlwch;
  2. Stay on the A5052 all the way to Brynrefail;
  3. Take the first left - just before the craft shop, with the yellow sign;
  4. Follow the road for about 2 miles;
  5. When you see the large hill on your right, look for a stone block with the words 'Parc Pen', on the left hand side of the road.
  6. Follow the dirt track until you reach the car park, and a member of staff will be there to meet you.
